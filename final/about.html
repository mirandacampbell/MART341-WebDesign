Who is Miranda?

My name is Miranda Campbell and I currently reside in Missoula, Montana. I will soon graduate from the University of Montana with a Bachelors Degree in Psychology. When I am not studying you can find me working at St. Patrick Hospital as a CNA on the Surgical/Pediatric Unit. However, I believe that everyone needs a hobby outside of work and school and mine just happens to be baking. I love trying new recipes and like to challenge myself with different types of decorating techniques. The home page displays two of my favorite fall recipes, and I would definitely recommend you give them a try! I always love any opportunity to bake treats for holidays and or any other special occasions. Please visit the Contact Page to send me some of your favorite recipes!
